LANDSCAPING TIPS

Plan before you buy.

Sketch your landscape design on paper before you start planting and building.

Knowing exactly what you need and where you're going to put it helps avoid wasting money.

Buy in phases if you have the financial resources to landscape their property all at once. Divide your project into phases, and pay as you go with funds on hand.

Evaluate your progress and adjust plans before moving to the next phase.

Check at your local specialty shop because they may provide more personal service, expert advice, and guarantees, which are all helpful if you're a novice.

Don't assume cheaper is always better.
